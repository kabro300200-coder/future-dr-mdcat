## 1. Information Collection - Overview

Future Dr. is an educational app for MDCAT medical entry test preparation. We respect your privacy and collect **ONLY** anonymized data for analytical purposes.

## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)
- We use Google AdMob to display ads
- AdMob may collect: Advertising ID, device information, IP address
- **Purpose:** To show relevant ads that support free app development
- **No personalized ads** are served without consent

### 2.2 Firebase (Analytics Only)
- We use Google Firebase **ONLY for analytics and crash reporting**
- Firebase collects: App usage data, device model, OS version, crash reports
- **Purpose:** To understand how users interact with the app and fix bugs
- **No user tracking across other apps or websites**

### 2.3 Local Storage (Your Device Only)
- Your quiz progress and scores are stored **ONLY on your device**
- This data is never uploaded to any server
- You can delete this data by clearing app storage

## 3. What We DO NOT Collect

❌ No name, email, or phone number  
❌ No account registration required  
❌ No location data (GPS)  
❌ No contacts, SMS, or call logs  
❌ No photos or media files  
❌ No browsing history

## 4. How We Use Your Data

| Data Type | Purpose | Shared With |

## 7. User Rights & Controls

### Opt-out of Ads Personalization:
- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn off

### Delete Your Local Data:
- Go to device Settings → Apps → Future Dr. → Storage → Clear Data
